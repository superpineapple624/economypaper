## 中文摘要

注册制改革持续推进后，做市商制度已成为完善科创板市场功能的重要安排。科技企业普遍面临信息不对称较强、估值难度较大、股价波动较高等问题，因此，做市商制度能否提高股票定价效率，不仅关系到市场资源配置功能，也关系到科创板制度优化的实际效果。研究这一问题，有助于理解市场微观交易机制对价格形成的影响。

本文以科创板上市公司为样本，选取2020年1月至2025年12月的公司月度面板数据，结合做市标的分批纳入的制度特征，构建多期DID模型和事件研究模型。以价格延迟为主要指标，并以股价同步性和残差波动率作为替代指标，考察做市商制度对定价效率的影响。模型中控制公司特征变量、公司固定效应和年月固定效应，标准误按公司层面聚类，并通过缩尾处理、随机化安慰剂检验和分组回归进行稳健性与异质性分析。

研究结果表明，科创板做市商制度总体上有助于改善科技企业定价效率。基准回归显示，价格延迟指标下制度效应方向稳定，股价同步性指标下改善作用更为明显，说明做市商制度有助于促进公司特质信息进入股票价格。事件研究结果表明，处理组与对照组在做市前不存在显著趋势差异，平行趋势假设总体成立，制度效应更可能呈现逐步释放的特征。稳健性检验结果与基准回归基本一致。异质性分析进一步发现，小市值企业和低信息透明度企业的改善倾向更明显，表明做市商制度在信息摩擦较强的企业中更容易发挥积极作用。

关键词：科创板；做市商制度；定价效率；多期DID；股价同步性

## English Abstract

As the registration-based IPO system continues to advance and the STAR Market further improves its institutional framework, whether the market maker mechanism can enhance the pricing efficiency of technology firms has become an important theoretical and practical question. Technology firms are often characterized by greater information asymmetry, higher valuation uncertainty, and stronger stock price volatility. Whether prices can reflect firm-level information more fully and more promptly therefore matters directly for the resource allocation function of the capital market. Examining the effect of the market maker mechanism on pricing efficiency can thus deepen our understanding of how market microstructure shapes price formation and provide evidence for the further optimization of the STAR Market.

Using monthly panel data on STAR Market listed firms from January 2020 to December 2025, this paper exploits the staggered inclusion of firms into the market making list and constructs a multi-period difference-in-differences model together with an event-study specification. Price delay is used as the baseline measure of pricing efficiency, while stock price synchronicity and residual volatility are adopted as alternative measures for robustness checks. The regressions control for firm characteristics, firm fixed effects, and year-month fixed effects, with standard errors clustered at the firm level. Additional analyses, including winsorization, a randomization placebo test, and subgroup regressions, are conducted to assess robustness and heterogeneity.

The results show that the STAR Market market maker mechanism overall helps improve the pricing efficiency of technology firms. In the baseline regressions, the estimated effect is directionally stable under the price delay measure, while the improvement is more evident when stock price synchronicity is used, suggesting that the market maker mechanism helps more firm-specific information enter stock prices. The event-study results support the parallel-trend assumption and indicate that the policy effect is more likely to emerge gradually rather than instantaneously. The robustness checks are broadly consistent with the baseline findings, suggesting that the main conclusions are reasonably stable. Further heterogeneity analysis shows that the improvement is more pronounced among small-cap firms and firms with lower information transparency, indicating that the market maker mechanism is more likely to play a positive role where information frictions are stronger.

Keywords: STAR Market; market maker mechanism; pricing efficiency; multi-period DID; stock price synchronicity
